A Personal Welcome

It is my great pleasure to welcome you to The Grand Lakeview, where tradition meets luxury. Our family has been serving guests with warmth and hospitality for over two decades, creating unforgettable experiences in the heart of Pune.

Every detail at The Hotel Lakeview has been carefully curated to reflect our passion for excellence and our deep respect for local heritage. From the architecture to the cuisine, we strive to offer you an authentic taste of Pune's royal legacy.
