About Hotel

The Grand Tulip, a business class luxury hotel, located in the heart of Pune city, follows its legacy from Hotel Tushar already a symbol of true Indian hospitality since 1984. The Grand Tulip offers high level of service and hospitality as well as wide range of amenities and upscale facilities creating a warm inviting environment.
